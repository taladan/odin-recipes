Egg Drop Soup Recipe

Description

A simple, hearty, warming bowl of tasty egg drop soup to start your meal off right.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken broth, divided
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄8 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 11⁄8 tablesppons cornstarch
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk

Steps

  1. Reserve 3/4 cup of chicken broth, and pour the rest into a large saucepan. Stir in chives, salt, and ginger; bring to a rolling boil. Stir reserved 3/4 cup of broth and constarch until smooth. Set aside.
  2. Whisk eggs and egg yolk together in a small bowl with a fork. Using a fork, drizzle eggs, a little at a time, into boiling broth. Eggs will cook immediately. Stir in cornstarch mixture gradually until soup reaches desired consistency.
